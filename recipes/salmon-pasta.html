Salmon Pasta

Description

This salmon and lemon pasta recipe is a bright, flavorful dish that brings together the richness of salmon with the zesty freshness of lemon. A light, creamy sauce made with onion, lemon zest, and a splash of cream coats each strand, balancing the buttery salmon with a refreshing citrus lift.

Ideal for a quick weeknight dinner or a casual yet elegant meal, this pasta is satisfying without being heavy. Whether you're using fresh or leftover salmon, the dish comes together quickly and can be easily customized with ingredients like spinach, capers, or chili flakes for a bit of heat.

Ingredients

  • 250g spaghetti
  • 300g smoked salmon
  • 1 onion
  • 250ml cream
  • 1 lemon
  • salt
  • pepper
  • Vegetal oil

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of water to boil and then add a generous amount of salt.
  2. Add the spaghetti and cook until al dente.
  3. Cut the salmon and the onion into bite-sized pieces, zest the lemon and squeeze to gather the juice, set ingredients aside
  4. Heat a tablespoon of oil in a frying pan and sauté the onion.
  5. Add the salmon and lemon zest and sauté for a few minutes.
  6. Add the cream, 2 tablespoons lemon juice and pepper and cook for 5 to 10 minutes.
  7. When the pasta is cooked, toss with the sauce. Serve with a sprinkling of Parmesan cheese.
